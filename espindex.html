bio

i am a chilean writer, workshop teacher and (casual) programmmer with an ugly bent towards nostalgia. my perpetual restlessness always has me looking for creative projects to work on. i have a particular interest in poetry, digital literature and essay writing, though i also engage in fiction writing, collage and literary editing among other fields and mediums. i have published many of my works in zine format, both in spanish and english, and i have published a poetry anthology with alien buddha press, which you can get here.

poesía


rojo

     Las flores muertas

   las rodillas rasmilladas

 el vestido quemado

 la gracia estúpida

 de todo aquello

   que nunca

 pudimos prevenir.

  tragando girasoles,

el viento teñido de rojo,

 y jamás levantando la mirada

    entendimos

                    por fin

la cartografía

de la pérdida
